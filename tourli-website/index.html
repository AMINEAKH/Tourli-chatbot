What are the best restaurants in Fes?

Fes has incredible culinary traditions! Try Restaurant Fez City for authentic Moroccan cuisine, or Dar Roumana for a boutique dining experience in a renovated riad. Both are highly rated and offer traditional ambiance with modern cuisine.

How far is Essaouira from Casablanca?

Essaouira is approximately 360 km west of Casablanca, about 4-5 hours by car. It's a beautiful coastal city perfect for a day trip or weekend escape. I'd recommend the scenic Atlantic coast drive!
